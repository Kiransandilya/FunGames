HOW TO PLAY

Two light cycles race around the grid, each leaving a solid wall of light behind it. You can't stop and you can't reverse — only turn.

Crash into any wall — the edge, your own trail, or your rival's — and you're out. Last cycle riding wins the round. First to 5 takes the match.

Player 1 steers with W A S D. Player 2 steers with the arrow keys. On a phone, swipe to steer in 1‑player mode.

Pick 1 Player to duel the AI, or 2 Players to grab a friend on the same keyboard. The cycles speed up the longer a round lasts — don't get boxed in.

Play Online connects you to a faraway friend: pick a room code, share the link, and you both steer with the arrow keys on your own screens. (Online needs the hosted page — not a local file.)

Difficulty sets the pace and how sharp the AI plays: Easy is slow and forgiving; Expert is fast and hunts you down — it'll cut off your space, not just survive.

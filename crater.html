CRATER DUEL

artillery · carve the earth

HOW TO PLAY

Two tanks, rolling terrain, and gravity. On your turn, pick a weapon, then drag from your tank to set angle & power and release to fire — arc the shell over the hills and the wind.

A hit damages the enemy by how close it lands, and carves a crater in the ground — reshape the battlefield, dig them out, or bury yourself. Drop the enemy to 0 HP to win the round; first to 3 rounds wins.

1 Player duels the AI (Easy→Expert). 2 Players shares one mouse — pass it on each turn. Play Online connects a faraway friend by room code; turn-based, so no lag.

Weapons: Shot (reliable) · Big Shot (huge blast) · Cluster (three bomblets) · Digger (carves deep, little damage).

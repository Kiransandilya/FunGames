LONGBOW DUEL

draw the bow · mind the wind

P1 0 — 0 P2
Drag from your archer to aim & set power · release to loose · first to 5 hits

HOW TO PLAY

Two archers, opposite ends of the field. On your turn, drag from your archer in the direction you want to fire — the further you drag, the more power. Release to loose a single arrow on an arc.

Gravity pulls it down and the wind (shown up top) pushes it sideways — read both. A hit scores a point; first to 5 wins. Miss and the turn passes.

1 Player duels the AI (Easy→Expert). 2 Players shares one mouse/touch — pass it on each turn. Play Online connects a faraway friend by room code; you take turns over the wire (no lag to worry about — it's turn-based).

Keyboard also works on your turn: ↑/↓ angle, ←/→ power, Space to fire.
